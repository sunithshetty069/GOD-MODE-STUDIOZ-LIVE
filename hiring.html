Who We Are

we're a little obsessed.
okay very obsessed.

About cinema. About craft. About getting it right. You'll fit right in.

GodMode Studioz is India's first multimedia production house built exclusively for films. We are the team behind the trailers, teasers, motion graphics and campaign visuals of some of the biggest blockbusters in Indian cinema across 7 languages.

We don't make content. We make people feel things. And right now we're looking for a few more obsessed people.

The Work
Real films. Real scale.

You will be working alongside a team that has been part of blockbuster films of Indian cinema. The kind of work that moves millions before a film even releases.

The Place
This isn't a workplace.

It's where film-obsessed people finally feel at home. Loud opinions about cinema are encouraged. Random film references in meetings happen daily. Passion over everything.

The Studio
A homecoming.

Netflix. Amazon Prime. Hombale Films. Jio Studios. We work with the biggest names in the industry. If your obsession is cinema, this is where your career belongs.
